Environmental Welfare

We incorporate high quality ecological textiles and recycled cardboard into our packaging, establishing the tea shop as an eco-friendly company.

Every set of teabags is wrapped in organic cotton batiste before being packed into box packages made from recycled cardboard to ensure our tea gets to you safe and sound without hurting our environment.
